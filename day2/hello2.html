eshadripuram College, established in 1973 under the Seshadripuram Educational Trust in Bengaluru, began with just 30 students and has grown into a reputed institution with over 3,000 students. It is affiliated with Bengaluru City University and accredited with an A++


grade by NAAC. The urban campus spans 2 acres, equipped with modern classrooms, a digital library, computer labs, and cultural activity spaces. The college offers various undergraduate programs like B.Com, BBA, BCA, and postgraduate courses including

M.Com, MBA, and MCA. It is known for its academic excellence, strong placements, and holistic development through co-curricular and extracurricular activities.
