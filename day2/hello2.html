About My Computer


HP (Hewlett-Packard) is one of the most well-known and trusted names in the computer industry. Founded in 1939, HP started as an electronics company in a garage in Palo Alto, California, and has since grown into a global technology giant. HP computers are known for their durability, innovative features, and wide range of models that cater to both personal and professional needs.


HP offers a variety of computers including desktops, laptops, all-in-one PCs, and workstations. Some of its most popular laptop series include HP Pavilion, HP Envy, HP Spectre, and the HP Omen gaming series.
